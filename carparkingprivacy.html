Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Car Parking: Find My Car (“the App”) handles your information. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Our guiding principle is simple: all data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal data (name, email, phone number)
  • No location history stored on servers
  • No analytics, advertising identifiers, or usage tracking
  • No third-party data sharing or sales

Information Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Your saved parking location (GPS coordinates)
  • Notes you add (e.g., floor, section, landmark)
  • Reference photos you capture within the App

This information is never transmitted to external servers. It remains on your device unless you choose to delete it or share it manually (for example, exporting a photo).

Permissions Used

  • Location (While Using the App): Used only to save your parking spot and guide you back. We do not track location in the background.
  • Camera / Photos (Optional): Used only to attach a reference photo of your parking location. Images stay on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by removing saved spots in the App or by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of children’s data being misused.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the date above and publish the updated version on this page before releasing any new version of the App that changes how data is handled.
