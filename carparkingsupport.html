Getting Started

  1. Open the app after parking.
  2. Allow Location access when prompted (While Using the App on iOS).
  3. Tap Save Spot to store your current location.
  4. Add an optional note (level, section, landmark).
  5. Tap Photo to attach a quick reference image (optional).
  6. When ready to return, tap Find to navigate back.

Accuracy Tips (iOS)

  • Ensure Precise Location is enabled: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → App → Precise Location
  • Set location access to While Using the App.
  • Step outdoors or near open areas for a faster GPS lock.
  • Keep Low Power Mode off when saving a spot for best accuracy.

Privacy First

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. All information is stored locally on your device and never leaves it unless you choose to share it (e.g., exporting a photo from your library).

  • Data Storage: Your saved spots, notes, and photos are stored only on your device.
  • No Collection: We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location in the background.
  • Deletion: Delete spots in‑app or uninstall the app to remove all local data.

For App Store disclosure: “Data Not Collected.” If these policies change, this page will be updated before any release.

Permissions We Request

  • Location (While Using the App): to save where you parked and guide you back. We never store or transmit your location to servers.
  • Camera / Photos (Optional): to attach a reference photo. Images remain on your device.

Troubleshooting

  • Spot not saving? Check Location permissions and Precise Location are enabled.
  • Blue dot inaccurate? Move to an open area and wait a few seconds for GPS to settle.
  • Directions not opening? Ensure you have an internet connection for map routing.
  • Photo access denied? Allow Camera/Photos in Settings → Privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions
