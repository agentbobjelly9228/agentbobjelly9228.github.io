Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2025

At MemoryFun, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

MemoryFun is designed with privacy in mind:

  • ✅ We do not collect or store any personal data.
  • ✅ We do not track your location.
  • ✅ We do not maintain user profiles on servers.
  • ✅ We do not store your photos or gameplay data remotely.

2. Photo Library Access

MemoryFun may request permission to access your photo library. This access is used **only to allow you to select personal photos** for use in the memory matching game.

Photos selected for gameplay remain on your device and are not stored externally.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. Period.

4. Children’s Privacy

MemoryFun is suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect any personal information from users, including children.

5. App Analytics

MemoryFun does not use any analytics tools that collect identifiable information. We may view anonymous aggregate data (e.g., total installs) provided by app store platforms.

6. Your Rights

Since MemoryFun does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. You remain in full control of your photos and app usage at all times.
