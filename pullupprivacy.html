Overview

About Pull Ups: Training Program

Pull Ups: Training Program (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a fitness app with 6 guided programs, clear exercise instructions and diagrams, a built-in rest timer, and achievement badges to help you stay consistent.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. The app is designed to work without creating an account and stores most data on your device.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Support Emails: If you email us, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.

We do not require an account and we do not collect workout logs or health data.

Information We Collect Automatically

Diagnostics: Crash reports and basic performance data may be collected to improve stability.

Usage Analytics (optional): We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which screens are used, without storing personally identifying information. You can opt out where applicable in app settings.

Device Data: Device model, OS version, and app version to help diagnose issues.

On-Device Storage

Program progress markers and earned badges are stored on your device. If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed from your device. Your device backups (e.g., iCloud/iTunes) may include this data under your control.

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

Provide Core Features: Power the guided programs, show instructions/diagrams, and run the rest timer.

App Quality: Improve performance, reliability, and usability based on diagnostics and (optional) analytics.

Support: Respond to your inquiries when you contact us.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your data. We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone data. We do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

Information Sharing

Service Providers

We may use third-party providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) to operate and improve the app. These providers are permitted to process data only to perform services on our behalf and must protect it appropriately.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

Business Transfers

If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

App Permissions

Notifications & Haptics

The rest timer can use notifications, sounds, or vibration/haptics. You can control these in the app settings and your device settings.

The app does not require access to your camera, photos, microphone, or location.

Data Security

Protection Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data we process (e.g., encryption in transit, access controls, and periodic reviews). No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Data

Locally stored program flags and badges remain on your device until you delete the app or reset data.

Support emails are retained as needed to address your request and maintain records, unless you request deletion where applicable.

Your Rights and Choices

Access & Deletion

You can delete the app to remove on-device data. For any server-side data (e.g., support emails), contact us to request access or deletion, subject to legal obligations.

Children's Privacy

Age Requirements

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us to request deletion.

International Data Transfers

Global Services

Service providers used by the app may process data in other countries. Where applicable, we take steps to ensure adequate protection consistent with relevant laws.

Changes to This Policy

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in-app or via other appropriate means.

Your continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
