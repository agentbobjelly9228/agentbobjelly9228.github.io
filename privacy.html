Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At MacroMagic–AI, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, collect, and protect your data when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We designed MacroMagic–AI with privacy in mind. That means:

  • ✅ We do not collect personal data.
  • ✅ We do not track your location.
  • ✅ We do not store any user profiles on our servers.

2. Local Device Storage

All of your food logs, photos, and custom settings are stored **locally on your device** and are never uploaded to a remote server unless you explicitly back them up using your device’s native backup options.

3. Camera & Photo Access

The app may request permission to access your camera or photo library, solely to scan or analyze food. We do not use this access for any other purpose, and no media files are uploaded or stored remotely.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to any third parties. Period.

5. Children’s Privacy

MacroMagic–AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years old.

6. App Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (e.g., Apple App Store metrics) to understand general usage trends. These analytics do not identify or track individuals.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is no data for us to retrieve or delete. You are always in control of your own information on your device.
